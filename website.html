Why Are Ice Shelves Melting

Human activities are a factor. Ever since the industrial revolution, carbon dioxide and other greenhouse gas emissions have raised temperatures, becoming even higher in the poles and as a result, glaciers are rapidly melting, calving off into the sea and retreating on land. The result of the ice shelves melting is that it removes a protection for glaciers because ice shelves protect from large masses of ice to go into the ocean which affects sea level. An obvious thing that causes ice shelves to melting is that there is global warming and when the ice shelves come in tact with warm air created by greenhouse gasses it melts bit by bit.
